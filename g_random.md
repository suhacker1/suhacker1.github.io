## Philanthropy 
I recommend contributing to: 
+ [Housing Rights Initiative (HRI)](https://housingrightsny.org/) - protect tenant rights and preserve affordable housing in New York City
+ [One Nation's Emergency Village in Syria Project](https://onenationuk.org/projects/emergency-village-in-syria) - build brick homes for displaced Syrians
+ [GiveDirectly](https://www.givedirectly.org/) - provide unconditional cash transfers to those in need
